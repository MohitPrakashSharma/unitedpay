About Us

Company Profile:

Unitedpay, a brand of Primware Technologies Pvt Ltd., stands as a prominent entity in the financial technology sector. Its mission revolves around leveraging technology aligned with the India Stack to foster financial and digital inclusion. Over the years, Unitedpay has witnessed substantial growth, expanding its reach to encompass a big network of merchants and corporate entities.

Offering a comprehensive array of payment and onboarding solutions, Unitedpay provides solutions such as AEPS, Cash Management, BBPS, IMPS, BHIM Aadhaar Pay, UPI, MicroATM, and QR services. Moreover, Unitedpay has forged strategic partnerships with corporate Business Correspondent (BC) and a Master Merchant. These collaborations have contributed to the establishment of a robust backend infrastructure, ensuring resilience and efficiency in operations.

Vision :

At Unitedpay, we envision a future where financial transactions are not just transactions; they are experiences that empower and connect individuals and businesses. Our vision is to:
1. Empower Financial Inclusion:
- Extend banking and financial services to the underbanked and unbanked population, promoting financial inclusion across communities.
2. Innovate with Technology:
- Continuously embrace and integrate cutting-edge technology to provide secure, efficient, and user-friendly financial solutions.
3. Forge Trusted Partnerships:
- Build enduring relationships with our clients, partners, and stakeholders based on trust, transparency, and mutual success.
4. Be a Catalyst for Growth:
- Serve as a catalyst for economic growth by facilitating seamless and accessible financial transactions that drive individual and business prosperity.
5. Champion Security and Compliance:
- Uphold the highest standards of security and compliance in financial transactions, ensuring the trust and confidence of our users.

Our Commitment:

At United Pay, we are committed to delivering excellence in financial services, combining innovation, security, and user-centric design. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to provide a comprehensive suite of solutions that cater to the diverse needs of our clients, enabling them to navigate the financial landscape with confidence.
Join us on this journey towards a future where financial transactions are not just transactions; they are transformative experiences that make a positive impact on individuals, businesses, and society at large.
