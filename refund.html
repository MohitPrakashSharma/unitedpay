Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last Updated on March 23,2024

1. Introduction

This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds and cancellations are handled by Primware Technologies Private Limited ("the Company"). By using our services, you agree to this policy.

2. Definitions

- Transaction: A financial action involving the transfer of funds between parties.
- Refund: The process of returning funds to a customer after a transaction has been completed.
- Cancellation: The process of stopping or reversing a transaction before it has been completed.

3. Eligibility for Refunds

- Erroneous Transactions: Refunds may be issued for transactions processed in error, including duplicate payments or incorrect amounts.
- Fraudulent Transactions: If a transaction is identified as fraudulent, a refund will be provided upon verification.
- Service Failures: Refunds may be provided if a service failure occurs, resulting in an incomplete or unsuccessful transaction.

4. Refund Request Process

- Request Submission: Customers must submit a refund request within 7 days of the transaction date. Requests can be made through [email, customer portal, phone, etc.].
- Required Information: The refund request must include the transaction ID, date, amount, and reason for the refund.
- Processing Time: Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after the request is approved.

5. Non-Refundable Transactions

- Completed Services: Once a service has been fully rendered, the transaction is non-refundable unless explicitly stated otherwise in the service agreement.
- Regulatory Fees: Any fees imposed by regulatory bodies are non-refundable.
- Third-Party Fees: Fees charged by third parties involved in the transaction process are non-refundable.

6. Cancellation Policy

- Pre-Authorization Cancellations: Transactions can be canceled without charge before they are authorized or completed.
- Post-Authorization Cancellations: After authorization, cancellations may only be processed if the funds have not been transferred to the recipient's account.


7. Dispute Resolution

- Internal Review: Disputes will be reviewed by our internal team, and a decision will be communicated within 15 business days.
- Third-Party Arbitration: If a resolution cannot be reached, the dispute may be escalated to a third-party arbitration service as outlined in our Terms and Conditions.

8. Policy Changes

The Company reserves the right to modify this Refund and Cancellation Policy at any time. Customers will be notified of significant changes [via email, on the website, etc.]. Continued use of our services after changes have been made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
